Nico Mizono

About

Nico is born and raised in San Francisco, CA. He attended high school at Saint Ignatius College Prep. where he cultivated a love for sports, mainly basketball, soccer and golf. His love for sports continues to this day as he is active in an organized basektball and soccer league, and works with his high school alumni association through his class officer alumni position.

He currently works in San Francisco's booming tech scene as a mobile and web designer, and spends his free time taking pictures, cooking and staying in shape. He has one older brother, Max, who is a criminal defense attorney, and two wonderful parents who are enjoying their retirement.

First thing about me is that I love design and have a keen interest in aesthetics. I take pictures regularly and love producing beautiful images.
I also love to chill out and enjoy my downtime. In my spare time I play hoops, get outdoors, and travel whenever I get the chance.
At heart I am a competitive dude. I love sports and winning, being around friends and approaching life with a craftsman mentality.
